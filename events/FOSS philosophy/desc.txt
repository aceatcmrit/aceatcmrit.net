<P STYLE="margin-bottom: 0cm"><B>Objectives:</B> 
</P>
<P STYLE="margin-left: 1.25cm; margin-bottom: 0cm">A guest lecture
organized by ACE, was presented by FSMK member,
 Mr. Naveen Mudunuru
on the understanding of FOSS PHILOSOPHY to
 the students of 4th
semester, CSE at CMRIT.
</P>
<P STYLE="margin-bottom: 0cm"><B>Event Description:</B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-left: 1.25cm; margin-bottom: 0cm; font-weight: normal">
Mr.Naveen addressed issues and brought to light the drawbacks
 within
proprietary soft wares that can be addressed using the kernel
support provided in GNU/Linux based operating systems.
</P>
<P STYLE="margin-left: 1.25cm; margin-bottom: 0cm; font-weight: normal">
The seminar could be termed as a key to mutual growth as CMRIT ACE
GLUG, an initiative of the Computer Science department at CMRIT is
working towards improving the knowledge of the students.
</P>
<P STYLE="margin-bottom: 0cm"><B>Event Results:</B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-left: 1.25cm; margin-bottom: 0cm"><SPAN STYLE="font-weight: normal">The
Students were given a proper opportunity to understand the
 necessity
and importance of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS).
 The various
issues that proprietary software does not address.
</SPAN></P>
